# Data Deletion Policy — StuGram

**Last updated:** 2026-05-18  
**Live URL:** [https://stugram.uz/delete-data — TO BE PUBLISHED BEFORE LAUNCH]

This page describes how to delete your StuGram account and what happens to your data.

---

## In-App Account Deletion (Primary Method)

**Required by Google Play Store (effective November 2023).**

1. Open StuGram
2. Go to your **Profile**
3. Tap the settings icon (top right)
4. Scroll to **Delete Account**
5. Tap **Delete Account** and confirm in the dialog
6. Your account is immediately deactivated

**What happens immediately:**
- Your profile is anonymised (name changed to "Deleted User", bio and avatar removed)
- All active sessions are revoked — you are logged out on all devices
- Your profile disappears from search results and public views

**What happens within 90 days:**
- All your posts, stories, messages, comments, and media are permanently deleted
- Your account record is purged from our database
- Media files are deleted from Cloudinary CDN

**What is retained indefinitely:**
- Anonymised audit logs required for legal/security compliance (no PII)
- Aggregate statistics (e.g. post count history) with no identifying information

**Subject:** Account Deletion Request  
**Include:** Your registered email address or username

We will process your request within **30 days** and confirm deletion by email.

---

## What Cannot Be Deleted

- **Your messages in group conversations** may remain visible as "Deleted User" sent a message — the content is deleted but the message slot may persist for conversation integrity
- **Audit logs** with anonymised identifiers required by law
- **Legal hold** — if your account is subject to a law enforcement request, deletion may be delayed

---

## Data Portability (Before Deletion)

Before deleting your account, you may request a copy of your data by emailing [support@stugram.uz — TO BE SET]. We will provide a data export within 30 days.
